## 1. Refund & Return Policy

> **Wapsi Aasaan Hai — Returns Made Simple**

We want every order to make you happy. If something doesn't, here's exactly how we make it right.

### Return window

You may request a return within **7 days of delivery** for any unused, undamaged item in its original packaging. The clock starts on the day the courier marks your order as delivered.

### How refunds are paid

- **Prepaid orders** (UPI, card, net-banking, wallet): refunded to the source account within **5-7 business days** of the returned item being received and inspected at our warehouse.
- **Cash on Delivery (COD) orders**: refunded via UPI to a number you provide, within **24 hours** of the returned item being picked up by our courier.

### Damaged, defective, or wrong item

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, you have **48 hours from delivery** to file a claim. Email a clear photo of the issue (including the courier packaging, if visible) to **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]** or message **[WHATSAPP_NUMBER]**. We will offer a **full replacement OR a full refund** — your choice — and arrange a return pickup at our cost.

### What's not eligible for return

- Opened personal-care or hygiene products (for safety reasons)
- Items missing original packaging, tags, or accessories
- Products damaged due to misuse after delivery
- Final-sale or clearance items, where clearly marked on the product page

### Return shipping costs

- **Free** if the return is due to our error (damaged, defective, wrong item).
- **₹100 deducted** from the refund if the return is for change-of-mind (this covers our courier pickup cost).

### How to start a return

WhatsApp **[WHATSAPP_NUMBER]** or email **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]** with your order number and reason. We'll arrange a free pickup from your address within 2-4 business days. No trips to a courier office required.

### Cancellation before dispatch

You can cancel any order at no cost as long as it has not yet shipped. Reach out via WhatsApp or email — most orders ship within 24-48 hours, so contact us as soon as possible.

### Questions

WhatsApp: **[WHATSAPP_NUMBER]** · Email: **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]** · Hours: 10 AM - 7 PM IST, Mon-Sat.

## 2. Shipping Policy

> **Delivery Ki Poori Jankari — Everything About Shipping**

### Where we ship

Pan-India delivery — every state, every union territory, including remote pincodes.

### How long it takes

- **Standard delivery**: 4-7 business days from order confirmation to your doorstep
- **Remote pincodes** (Northeast, parts of J&K, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep): 7-10 business days
- **Festival windows** (Diwali, Holi, Independence Day, etc.): allow 2-3 extra days; couriers slow down during peak periods

### Shipping costs

- **Free shipping** on all prepaid orders **₹499 and above**
- **Flat ₹49** on prepaid orders below ₹499
- **₹49** COD handling fee (offsets our courier-side surcharge)

### Cash on Delivery (COD)

Available on orders **up to ₹1,499**. For orders above ₹999, we may call or message via WhatsApp to confirm before dispatch — please respond within 24 hours or your order will be cancelled automatically (this protects both of us from delivery failures).

### Tracking

Within **24 hours** of dispatch, you'll receive an SMS and email with the tracking link and AWB number. You can track in real time on the courier's site or by replying to our shipment email.

### Courier partners

We ship via leading courier partners including **Shiprocket** (auto-routed by best-pincode-fit) and **Delhivery** (direct, used for high-value orders). The specific courier is chosen at dispatch based on delivery speed and reliability for your pincode.

### What if no one is home?

The courier will attempt delivery up to **3 times** on consecutive days. If all attempts fail, the package is held at the local hub for 5 days, then returned to us. We'll WhatsApp/email you before automatic return so you can request a redelivery slot.

### Order changes after dispatch

Once an order is shipped, we cannot change the delivery address. Cancellation after dispatch is treated as a return — see our Refund & Return Policy.

### Questions

WhatsApp: **[WHATSAPP_NUMBER]** · Email: **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]**.

## 3. Privacy Policy

> **Aapka Data, Aapka Bharosa — Your Data, Your Trust**

EcomBudgetStore (operating from [STREET_ADDRESS], Sonipat, Haryana, [PIN_CODE], India) takes your privacy seriously. Here is exactly what we collect, how we use it, and what control you have.

### What we collect

- **Account & order data**: name, shipping address, billing address, phone number, email
- **Payment data**: handled entirely by our payment processors (Razorpay / PayU / Shopify Payments). We **never store** your raw card number, CVV, UPI PIN, or net-banking credentials. We retain only the last 4 digits and transaction reference for our records.
- **Order history**: products purchased, dates, amounts — used to power your account, support tickets, and (if you opt in) personalised marketing
- **Device & analytics data**: browser type, IP address, pages visited, time spent — collected via cookies and pixels (see below)
- **Communications**: WhatsApp / email exchanges between you and our support team

### How we use your data

- To fulfil your orders and provide customer support
- To prevent fraud and verify Cash on Delivery orders
- For tax compliance (we retain order data for **7 years** to comply with GST and Income Tax requirements)
- To send order updates (transactional — these can't be opted out of while you have active orders)
- For marketing **only if you opt in** at checkout or via email subscription

### Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and pixels for the site to function and to measure marketing performance. Specifically:
- **Shopify session cookies** — required; the site can't run without them
- **Meta Pixel + Conversions API** — used to measure ad effectiveness and show you relevant ads on Facebook and Instagram
- **Google Analytics 4** — anonymised traffic and behaviour analytics

You can opt out of marketing cookies via your browser settings. The site will continue to work.

### Who we share data with

- **Couriers** (Shiprocket, Delhivery, etc.) — name, address, phone for delivery
- **Payment processors** (Razorpay, PayU, Shopify Payments) — what they need to charge you
- **Meta Platforms** — pseudonymised event data (hashed email/phone) for ad measurement
- **Tax authorities** — when legally required
- **We never sell your data to third parties.**

### Your rights

You can request to **access**, **correct**, or **delete** your personal data at any time. Email **[SUPPORT_EMAIL]** with your request. We'll respond within 7 business days. Note: we cannot delete data we are legally required to retain for tax purposes (typically 7 years for invoices).

### Children

We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has shared data with us, contact us and we'll delete it.

### Updates

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email or a banner on the site. The version in effect at the time of your interaction applies.
